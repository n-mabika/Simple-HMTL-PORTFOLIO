The Best Movies According to Andile

My top movies to watch in your spare time

Home Alone

This is such a Christmas class. It brings all wonderful childhood memories during christmas day. I loved siting and watching Calvin being a menace every year.Good laughs. Most memorable Christmas movie to have ever been created.

UShaka Zulu

The movie is about the upbringing of a young boy destined to dethrone his father. Its a historical treasure to the Zulu Nation here in South Africa. Its brutal to watch but gives you the rich history of the Zulu Kingdom.

Van Damme-Kickboxer

Its an old martial-arts movie but one of the best to ever been created. The lead character is iconic and gave a fever dream to any young WWE fan.
